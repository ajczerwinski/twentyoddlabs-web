About

Twenty Odd Labs is an independent software studio based in Washington State. We build across categories — from family apps and productivity tools to games and whatever earns the next spot on the list.

Every app we ship is small, deliberate, and designed to actually get used. The name is a goal: twenty-odd apps, made carefully, one at a time.
